The Tiger And The Lion

A Tiger was making fun of the Lion one day for being so slow.

"Do you ever get anywhere?" he asked with a mocking laugh.

"Yes" replied the Lion "and I get there quicker than you think. I'll run you a race and prove it."

The Tiger was much alarmed at the idea of running a race with the Lion, but for the fun of the thing he agreed, So the Dog, who had consented to act as judge, marked the distance yelled, "example".

The Tiger was soon far out of sight, and to make the Lion feel very deeply how ridiculous it was for him to try a race with a Tiger , he went off the course to practice running for 2 hours until the Lion should catch up.

The Lion meanwhile kept going slowly but steadily, and after a time passd the place where the Tiger was running and when at last he did stop, the Lion was near the goal. The Tiger now ran his swiftest, but he could not overtake the Lion in time.

Moral of the story

"This is a meaningful message."
